Entry Points

The Five Doors

Every path leads here.

Five different problems. Five different entry points. One hallway. One destination.

Door 1
🚧

Construction Contractors

“I need to house my crew near the job site.”

You're managing a major healthcare build in a rural or remote BC community. Finding and keeping crew housed is eating your time, your budget, and your margins. Per diems are chaos. Turnover is a nightmare.

ERS handles it. One contract. Furnished, move-in ready units near the site. Single invoice. Your crew is supported — you stay focused on the build.

✓ Housed crew
✓ Reduced turnover
✓ One point of contact
✓ Single invoice
Door 2
🏥

Health Authorities

“I can't recruit staff because there's nowhere for them to live.”

You're running a regional hospital and the staffing crisis isn't abstract — it's beds diverted, surgeries delayed, agency costs through the roof. The problem isn't finding talent. It's that talent won't come somewhere with no housing.

ERS is the housing. Purpose-built, fully managed, close to your facility. Staff arrive ready to work. They stay because they're settled.

✓ Staff retention
✓ Reduced agency costs
✓ Housing your recruitment team can promote
Door 3
👩‍⚕️

Healthcare Professionals

“I want to take the posting, but I need somewhere to live.”

You've got an offer at a northern hospital. The pay is good. But furnished housing near the hospital? Nonexistent. Hotels are expensive. Long-term rentals are scarce. You've turned down postings before for exactly this reason.

ERS is why you say yes. Show up. Walk in. Start working.

✓ Furnished, move-in ready
✓ Near the hospital
✓ Stable, safe & supported
Door 4
🏢

Executives & Board Directors

“Our staffing numbers are a governance problem. I need a real solution.”

You're not managing shifts — you're managing a system. Turnover costs, agency dependency, recruitment failures, and service disruptions are board-level issues. You need a solution that shows up on the balance sheet, not just a band-aid.

ERS is the strategic housing partner your health authority has been missing.

✓ Measurable outcomes
✓ Retention rates & cost savings
✓ Something to bring to the board
Door 5
🏗️

Capital Partners

“I want to put capital into something recession-resistant and mission-driven.”

You're looking at real estate differently. Hotels feel exposed. Market condos are competitive. You want institutional-grade tenancy, strong fundamentals, and a story that holds up to scrutiny.

Healthcare housing around hospitals is the most defensible real estate in Canada. ERS brings the operator expertise, the health authority relationships, and the grassroots community platform.

✓ Proven operator
✓ Established HA relationships
✓ Grassroots community platform
✓ Purpose-built pipeline across BC
